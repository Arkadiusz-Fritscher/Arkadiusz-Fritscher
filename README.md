- 💬 A Story about me</br>
  **Hello, my name is Arkadiusz, and I am a self-taught frontend developer from near Mainz, Germany. My interest in programming was sparked as a child when I created simple HTML pages using Dreamweaver and Photoshop. After completing my training as an industrial mechanic, I was hired by the same company where I still work today. In 2019, I decided to revive my long-held dream of working as a programmer. Since then, I have been using every free moment alongside my full-time job and as a father of two young children to teach myself programming. I read, listen to podcasts, take online courses, and work on personal projects to enhance my knowledge, particularly in the frontend domain. I am enthusiastic about the possibilities of frontend development and look forward to expanding my skills and taking on new challenges. If you have any questions or are interested in collaborating, please feel free to contact me.**
